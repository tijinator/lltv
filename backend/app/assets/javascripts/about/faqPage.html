Membership / Plans / Group Memberships

  • How do I update my profile?
  • Click on your name drop down on the upper right top navigation bar and select “Profile”. Update all of your changes here.
  • What type of membership plans do you offer? How much do plans cost?
  • Currently, $24.92 per month (or one annual payment of $299) gives you unlimited access to our courses and learning modules on your computer, tablet, or mobile phone.
  • How can I change my membership plan?
  • Click on your name drop down and select “Account Settings”. Then on the left click on “Subscription Info”. You can change your plan here.
  • Is my credit card transaction secure?
  • The credit card information is you give us is protected by SSL technology and secured networks that are only accessible to our webmaster.
  • Do you offer free trials?
  • At this time, we do not offer free trials.
  • Do you offer group memberships or discounts for students / professional memberships?
  • At this time, we do not offer discounts.
  • Can I access LLTV from multiple devices?
  • Yes. Your subscription allows you to watch our learning videos on your computer, tablet, or mobile device.
  • How do I cancel my membership?
  • We’re sorry to see you go! If you need to cancel your membership please contact us at 917-588-6261.
  • What’s your privacy policy?
  • We will never rent, sell, or reveal to outside persons or companies any email list, personal or purchase information you have given us, without your permission.
